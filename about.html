About Living the Journey with Chase

Husband, Dad, Coach, Learner

I’m Chase – a husband, dad of two awesome girls, youth sports coach, and outdoor enthusiast who’s trying to grow a little bit every day. Between family time, coaching baseball and T-ball, working full-time, and sneaking in early-morning fishing trips, life is busy—but it’s also where the best lessons are.

This site is a place to capture those lessons. The wins, the losses, the funny stories from the dugout, and the real moments at home when parenting feels both amazing and overwhelming. I don’t have it all figured out—but I’m committed to living the journey with intention.

Why I Built This Website

I wanted a space away from social media’s highlight reels where real life could be shared honestly. My hope is that another parent, coach, or busy professional can visit this site and think, “I’m not the only one,” and leave with something encouraging or practical they can try this week.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Stories and reflections from family life and coaching youth sports.
  • Ideas for simple outdoor adventures and ways to get kids moving.
  • Honest thoughts about balancing work, school, and home.
  • Resources, tools, and links that have helped me grow along the way.

You’re invited to learn with me, not from someone who has all the answers, but from someone who’s in the middle of the journey right alongside you.
