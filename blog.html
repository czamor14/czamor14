Coaching Kids: Building Confidence Before Skill

Youth Sports • Coaching Mindset

Before we talk about throwing mechanics or swing paths, we have to help kids believe they belong on the field. In this post, I walk through three simple things I try to do every practice: call kids by name, celebrate effort, and let them fail safely. The scoreboard matters—but confidence lasts longer than any single game.
